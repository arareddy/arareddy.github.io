Biography

I am a first year PhD student at Northwestern University in the CS theory group and am presently advised by Anindya De. Before joining Northwestern, I was an undergraduate student at IIT Kanpur where I majored in Computer Science and Engineering and minored in Physics.

Broadly, I am interested in all areas of theoretical computer science. Presently, I am learning about Pseudorandom generators, Mechanism Design and Stochastic Analysis. I also have peripheral interests in Cryptography and Quantum Computing.
